About The Daily Worker

The Daily Worker is a news aggregation and commentary platform providing working-class perspectives on current events. We curate stories from mainstream sources and analyze them through the lens of labor rights, economic justice, and worker welfare.

Our Approach

All content is AI-assisted with human editorial oversight. We clearly distinguish between factual reporting (sourced from established news outlets) and our editorial analysis and opinion.
